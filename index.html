How to use ANU CS Assignment Analyse Helper

1. Add your background, if you have one

Search for completed ANU COMP courses, click Add course, and enter your mark from 0 to 100. First-semester students can skip this step.

2. Choose the assignment course

Find the course you want to analyse and click Analyze assignment on that course card.

3. Provide the assignment brief

Upload a TXT/PDF file or paste the assignment text directly into the text box.

4. Generate the estimate

The report estimates total hours, difficulty, confidence, covered knowledge, missing knowledge, and workload phases.

5. Review history

Past reports are saved in this browser's history panel. They are stored locally on your device.
